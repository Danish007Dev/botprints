How to read BotPrints

Risk Score (0-100): Higher score means more bot-like behavior.

User Stats:
• Posts / Comments: Volume of activity.
• Edits: Number of times they changed their posts/comments.
• Days Active: How long we've been tracking them.

Badges:
• ✓ Consistent: Normal, steady behavior.
• ⚡ Shift: Sudden change in posting patterns (possible account purchase).
• 🔗 Bot Ring: Posts at the exact same time as other accounts.

Signals (Radar Chart):
• TMP (Temporal): Unnatural posting speed.
• CRC (Circadian): Never sleeps, posts 24/7.
• ENG (Engage): Weird post-to-comment ratio.
• EDT (Edit): Edits content too frequently.
• BST (Burst): Long silence followed by sudden spam.
